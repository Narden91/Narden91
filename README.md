## 🧠 About Me

AI Scientist with a **PhD in Artificial Intelligence** focused on building **trustworthy AI systems for healthcare diagnostics**.

My research investigates how **human behavioral signals** — particularly handwriting dynamics — can act as **digital biomarkers** for early detection of neurological conditions such as Alzheimer's disease and Specific Learning Disorders.

I design **end-to-end intelligent systems**, combining:

- Agentic AI architectures
- Large Language Models (LLMs)
- Evolutionary computation
- Explainable AI (XAI)
- Multimodal medical data analysis

My goal is to move AI from *prediction* toward **clinical decision support and autonomous reasoning systems**.
